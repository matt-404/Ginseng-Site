Privacy Policy

Fong's Ginseng will not sell or share customer's information to any third parties. We collect information from our customers to process orders and to contact our customer with order confirmations. As well as, using for order status updates. Information collected includes your name, billing address, shipping address, telephone numbers, e-mail address, and payment information (Credit Card).
Your user name and password remain confidential, we DO NOT sell or share this information with anyone!

Registration

In order to process your orders, you are required to complete the registration form. You will be required to provide your contact information. This includes your name, street address, telephone number, and e-mail address. This information is used to provide services such as automated order status updates via e-mail from Fong's Ginseng.

Order

​Most of the information collected in the registration process will be used to process orders. You will have to provide financial information such as your credit card number, expiration date, etc. This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill your order.
If we have trouble processing an order, we will use this information to contact you. To process your credit card information, we must share your personal and financial information with the merchant bank for authorization and approval. This process is secured by an advance security system. We do not share your personal and financial information with any third parties!
